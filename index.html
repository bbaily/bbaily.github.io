About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at the University of Michigan.

Research Interests

I'm interested in commutative algebra and algebraic geometry in positive characteristic. Currently, I'm working on tensor ranks and on F-pure thresholds of complete intersections. My thesis advisor is Karen Smith.
